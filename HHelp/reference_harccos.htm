Harccos Function

Returns the inverse hyperbolic cosine of a number. Number must be greater than or equal to 1. The inverse hyperbolic cosine is the value whose hyperbolic cosine is number, so harccos(hcos(number)) equals number.

Syntax

harccos(number)

Number   is any valid numeric expression equal to or greater than 1.

Example

Expression Description (Result)
harccos(1) Inverse hyperbolic cosine of 1 (0)
harccos(10) Inverse hyperbolic cosine of 10 (2.9932)
