Exp Function

Returns e raised to a power.

Syntax

exp(number)

Number   is any valid numeric expression representing the exponent applied to the base e.

Remarks

  • To calculate powers of other bases, use the exponentiation operator (^).
  • exp is the inverse of ln, the natural logarithm of number.

Example

Expression Description (Result)
exp(1) Approximate value of e (2.7183)
exp(2) Base of the natural logarithm e raised to the power of 2 (7.3891)
